**Why this gig?**

Oh, you know the score by now. I've [seen Annie Eve so many times](https://gigs.dave.org.uk/tag/annie-eve/). I did consider not going to this show because a) it's only a couple of months since I last saw Annie and b) I really don't like the Borderline very much. But , in the end, I just couldn't resist it.

**Support**

I'd never heard of [Jake Morley](http://www.jakemorley.com/) before. He was very good. Folky singer-songwriter with a nice line in clever guitar techniques. Well worth checking out.
